About Us

We, are a team of third-year computer diploma students from Government Polytechnic Karad, consisting of Riya, Pratik, and Sharvani, have embarked on a microproject with the goal of raising awareness about extinct animals. To achieve this, we are developing a dedicated website.Our project revolves around studying extinct animals and creating a platform to share our findings with the world. Through our website, we aim to educate and inspire people to learn more about these fascinating creatures that once roamed our planet....

Extinct Animals

Basicallay Extinct animals means animals that no longer exist on Earth. This means they have all died, and there are no more of them left. Extinction happens when a species, which is a group of similar animals, disappears completely.Many different types of animals have become extinct throughout history. Some famous examples include the woolly mammoth, the dodo bird, and the saber-toothed tiger. But there are many more animals that have disappeared that we might not know about...
