ABOUT US

Title Of Micro-project:

"Study Of Extinct Animals: Our Microproject for Awareness"

Introduction:

We, are a team of third-year computer diploma students from Government Polytechnic Karad, consisting of Riya, Pratik, and Sharvani, have embarked on a microproject with the goal of raising awareness about extinct animals. To achieve this, we are developing a dedicated website.

Project Overview:

Our project revolves around studying extinct animals and creating a platform to share our findings with the world. Through our website, we aim to educate and inspire people to learn more about these fascinating creatures that once roamed our planet.We aim to inspire people to take action and make efforts to save endangered species. Together, we can make a positive impact and protect our precious wildlife!

Team Members:

Riya Sunil Kharade,Pratik Pramod Shejwal , Sharvani Bharat Mahajan

Institution:

We are students at Government Polytechnic Karad, currently pursuing our third-year computer diploma.

Objective:

Our primary objective is to create a user-friendly and informative website that sheds light on extinct animals, their history, and the reasons behind their extinction. By doing so, we hope to contribute to the preservation of our planet's biodiversity and promote environmental awareness.

Thank you for your interest in our microproject! Stay tuned for updates as we work diligently to bring this educational resource to life.
